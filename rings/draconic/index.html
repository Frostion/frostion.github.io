draconic webring

if your website features dragons, you yourself are a dragon, or you just love dragons, then the Draconic Webring is for you!

join the webring!

to join, just send me an email containing the following info about your site. all of this info will be displayed in the member directory below.

  • your website's URL, including http:// or https://
  • the name or title of your website
  • your (the webmaster's) name
  • a short description of your website

once i've added you to the ring, i'll let you know via email. please place the widget somewhere on your website as soon as possible after getting accepted!

add the widget to your site

the webring widget is a div that's 150px wide by 75px tall. add the following HTML to your page wherever you want the widget to show up:

<div id="draconic-widget"></div>

then, at the bottom of your HTML <body>, add this line to include the javascript that makes the widget work:

<script src="https://cyberdragon.digital/rings/draconic/widget.js"></script>
