Welcome to the Draconic Webring, hosted by Frost Sheridan.
If your website features dragons or you yourself are a dragon, then this webring is for you!

How to join the webring • View member directory

If you're interested in having your website be a part of the Draconic Webring, just send me an email at frostsheridan@posteo.net and include the following information about your site. This information will be displayed in the member directory.

  • Your website's URL, including http:// or https://
  • The name or title of your website
  • Your (the webmaster's) name
  • A short description of your website

Once I've added you to the ring, I'll let you know via email. Please place the widget somewhere on your website as soon as possible after getting accepted!

The widget is a div that's 150px wide by 90px tall. Add the following HTML to your page wherever you want the widget to show up:

<div id="draconic-widget"></div>

Then, at the bottom of your HTML <body>, add this line to include the javascript that makes the widget work:

<script src="https://www.cyberdragon.digital/rings/draconic/widget.js"></script>
