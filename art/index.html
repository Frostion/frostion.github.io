hyperunrealism.

Welcome to the future - infinitely far into the future. Time and space as you and I experience it has no meaning here. Computing technology has advanced to the point where it can now directly modify the fabric of the universe, meaning that physical reality effectively no longer exists - everything is digital now. Through my artwork, I explore what this post-reality universe of "hyperunrealism" could be like, where the separation between physical and virtual has entirely collapsed.

I primarily use Affinity Designer to create my artwork. Some pieces feature backgrounds that I've created using Bryce 7 or Processing.
