Physical Properties

 Overall
A cute, soft, fun tiger. What's not to love?

 Material
His vinyl is way softer and squishier than I thought it would be, especially for such an inexpensive toy.

 Appearance
Overall he looks nice, but the shape of his head is a bit weird and the insides of his legs are totally untextured (just solid white).

 Construction
His seams and overall craftsmanship are perfect.

 Durability
I haven't had him long enough yet to properly rate his durability.

 Cuddleability
He's a little unsteady when sitting on him and he's a bit too big to cuddle with in bed, but his softness makes up for it.
