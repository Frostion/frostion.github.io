flyynt

Species

Wolf (custom production run)

About

Flyynt is a custom-made toy that I managed to sneak into the preorders for just in time.

I'll address the bad parts first - the craftsmanship is...not good. His seams are messy, his snoot was put on crooked, and he had 3 leaks from the factory (luckily they were all very small and easy to repair). Sure, he was cheap by custom pool toy standards (I think he was only like $150 iirc), but still...I wasn't too happy.

HOWEVER, he's still a very good boy. He's super cute, very friendly, and amazing to cuddle with. The material he's made out of is ridiculously soft - it feels more like some kind of nylon fabric instead of vinyl, and much like Cirrus, he's a great little spoon. Personally I prefer regular, more plastic-feeling vinyl instead, but the unique material used is still pretty cool.

I also really really like his size. He's small enough to carry around and fit in my car so I can take him places without having to deflate him, and he's also just the right size for satisfying snuggles.

Physical Properties

 Overall
Even though this wolf is extremely cuddly, his build quality and durability leave a lot to be desired.

 Material
The vinyl is exceptionally soft and thin, and feels more like fabric than plastic. I much prefer 'traditional' vinyl though, but that's just personal preference.

 Appearance
He's super cute and the printing quality is good, but his snoot is crooked and the proportions of his face and ears look pretty strange.

 Construction
As mentioned above, the craftsmanship is the worst I've seen by far on any inflatable.

 Durability
Even though his seams are folded over and look pretty strong, he constantly gets leaks even though I'm careful with him.

 Cuddleability
His uniquely soft material and small size means that he's an excellent little spoon.
