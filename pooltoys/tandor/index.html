Physical Properties

 Overall
He looks a bit derpy, but he's very cuddly, well designed, and fun to be with.

 Material
His vinyl is a little thicker than the average inflatable, but it's still very soft and smooth (especially for a relatively inexpensive toy).

 Appearance
I love everything about his design, except for his face - he just looks a bit derpy I guess?

 Construction
His seams and overall craftsmanship are perfect.

 Durability
I haven't had him long enough to properly rate his durability (but it seems totally fine so far).

 Cuddleability
He's very sturdy compared to other raft-type pool toys and his vinyl is quite soft, so sitting and laying on him is very comfortable.
