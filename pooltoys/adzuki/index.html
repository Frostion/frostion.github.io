About

Adzuki (or just Zuki for short) is the most precious little dragon ever. He's cuddly, loving, curious, and just lots of fun to be with.

One of my favorite things about him is his size. He's much smaller than most inflatables, so he's able to be cuddled with just about anywhere, and he can tag along on trips and car rides!

Why the name Adzuki? Because, just look at him - he's such a tiny bean!

Physical Properties

 Overall
Definitely one of my favorites. He's super cute and tiny enough to carry with me everywhere!

 Material
Super shiny and super soft. Unlike most shiny inflatables, his vinyl feels nice and smooth rather than sticky or grippy.

 Appearance
Probably the cutest pool toy ever!

His seams and overall craftsmanship are completely perfect.

 Durability
I haven't had him long enough yet to properly rate his durability.

 Cuddleability
Because of his small size, he's great for cuddles in bed, on the couch, and anywhere else really!
