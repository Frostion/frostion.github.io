my pool toy hoard

Here's a list of everyone in my massive, ever-growing pool toy family.

cinder

Cinder's got the biggest personality out of all my dragons - he's charming, charismatic, and romantic as heck. He's not afraid to pull you into a surprise hug and never let you go.

adzuki

I was about to just name this little guy Bean, but then I realized how dumb that would be, so I did something infinitely better and named him after a cool-sounding species of bean instead. Zuki's a very extroverted, energetic little dragon and loves to meet new people (so that he can pester them for pets).

autumn

This big, soft, beautiful dragon is very loving and protective - she'll let anyone rest on her back and keep them warm and safe under her wings.

ember

Ember's big, strong, playful, and full of energy, but unlike Zuki, he's happy to calm down and let you use him as a bed. Him and Cinder have become good friends lately.

blue

Although Blue's shy around new people, she loves to travel and experience new things - she always likes to come along with me on big trips.

sage

Spherical dragons are the best dragons and Sage is no exception. He gives the biggest, squishiest hugs ever, and much like Cinder, he won't ever let you go once he's trapped you.

mist

Mist is shy, introverted, and looks super grumpy, but she can be soft and friendly once she gets to know you. It takes her a long time to warm up to new people though. She's usually sitting beside me and keeping me company while I'm at my computer working on projects.

tandor

I haven't been spending as much time with Tandor as I should be, so I don't have much to say about him yet, but he makes for a great bed at least.

tundra

Tundra's small size means that he can fit in my car fully inflated, so I'm able to easily drag him along on short trips and dates.

sparkles

This beautiful bean melted my heart the moment I first saw him. Unlike some of my other dragons, Sparkles doesn't really like rough cuddles - instead, he prefers to just calmly hang out.
