my pool toy hoard

Here's a list of everyone in my massive, ever-growing pool toy family.

adzuki

I was about to just name this little guy Bean (because he's such a bean), but then I realized how dumb that would be, so I did something infinitely better and named him after a cool-sounding species of bean instead. Zuki's an extremely extroverted and energetic little dragon who loves to meet new people (so that he can pester them for pets).

autumn

This big, soft, beautiful dragon is very loving and protective - she's happy to let you rest on her back and keep you warm and safe under her wings.

blue

Although Blue's shy around new people, she loves to experience new places and things - she always likes to come along with me whenever I travel.

cedar

Cedar was originally a flat raft/island-type inflatable, but his baffle separated some time ago, so now he's all big and round. I'm definitely not complaining though - I think he looks way better, plus it's a lot of fun to roll around and wrestle with him. He's super chill, laid-back, and always finds a way to be happy and content no matter what happens.

cinder

Cinder has the biggest personality out of all my dragons. He's charming, charismatic, romantic as heck, and he's not afraid to pull you into a surprise hug and never let you go. He's especially fond of Ember, and the two have become good friends lately.

cerisey

This adorable pink dragon just showed up, so I haven't had much time to get to know her yet.

ember

Ember's big, strong, playful, and full of energy, but he's also happy to calm down and snuggle up to you. He's also very heavy and protective, which makes him my preferred dragon to cuddle with when falling asleep.

mist

Mist is shy, introverted, and looks super grumpy, but she can be soft and friendly once she gets to know you (although it takes her a long time to warm up to new people, and she gets overstimulated easily). She usually sits beside me and keeps me company while I'm at my computer and/or working on projects.

sage

Spherical dragons are the best dragons, and Sage is no exception. He gives the biggest, strongest hugs ever, and much like Cinder, he won't ever let you go once he's trapped you.

sparkles

This beautiful bean melted my heart the moment I first saw him. Unlike some of my other dragons, Sparkles doesn't really like rough cuddles - instead, he prefers to just chill and hang out.

tandor

I haven't been spending as much time with Tandor as I should be, so I don't have much to say about him yet, but he makes for a great bed at least.

tundra

Because of Tundra's small size, he can fit in my car while fully inflated, so I'm able to easily drag him along on short trips and dates. Tundra enjoys being on the recieving end of rough cuddles - he loves being squished, laid on, and wrestled with.
