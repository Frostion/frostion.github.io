About

This chonker is so hecking adorable! He's big and soft enough to lay down on and cuddle with, or even fall asleep on. He's great to hang out with!

The material he's made out of isn't quite as soft and stretchy as more expensive toys, but it's not noticeable at all when snuggling with him. The overall build quality, durability, and artwork/printing are all top-notch. They could double the price on this toy and he would still be worth it.

You should totally adopt one of these guys - as long as you have the space! These Laprases (Laprii? idk) are definitely big (but that's not a bad thing at all).

Physical Properties

 Overall
One of my favorites for sure. He's big, extremely cute, and loves to cuddle.

 Material
Above average in softness and smoothness, but not quite as squishy as some toys.

 Appearance
One of the cutest inflatables I've seen, and the printing quality is great.

 Construction
His seams and overall craftsmanship are perfect.

 Durability
He's held up well to me laying on and snuggling with him, but his baffle seam is a little fragile, especially when tightly inflated.

 Cuddleability
His huge size and soft material make him a great bed.
