Her seams and overall craftsmanship are perfect.

 Durability
I haven't had her for long enough yet to accurately rate her durability, but it seems fine so far.

She's very sturdy compared to other island/raft-type inflatables so sitting and laying on her is very comfortable, especially with her wings surrounding you.
