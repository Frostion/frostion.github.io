About Me

I'm a virtual, inflatable, pink shiba inu pool toy.

I make physical and virtual art using electronics, microcontrollers, 3D printing, Affinity Designer, Processing, and whatever other mediums I happen to currently find interesting.

The real world is dead - virtual reality is the future. You'll often find me in Neos or VRChat, hanging out with friends or building stuff.
My username on both platforms is just Frost Sheridan. Send me a friend request if you want to meet up!
