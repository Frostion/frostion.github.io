about me

i'm frost, a dragon who's obsessed with keeping technology, design, and music from the late 90s and early 2000s alive and in use. if you come across a guy with a neon green cybiko, a neon purple MD walkman, and an absolute brick of a feature phone, then you've probably found me!

i love creating new stuff and hacking old stuff to work in new ways, so i'm always working on projects that involve digital art, computer graphics, programming, electronics, microcontrollers, 3D printing, VR, and whatever other mediums i happen to find interesting at the moment. i have a bit of a mystical/spiritual slant (especially in relation to tech), so my artwork and projects usually take on a weird, otherworldly, mystical vibe as well.

if you want to contact me, you can poke me on telegram, or add me on vrchat if you want to hang out sometime!
