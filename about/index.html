welcome to my digital den.

I'm Frost Sheridan, a digital mechadragon who exists outside of time. This website serves as a gallery of my various creative works, including photography, graphic design, electronics, and programming.

You can usually find me out taking photos with weird old cameras, tinkering with old Sony and Apple computers and tech, listening to music, hanging out in VRChat, and working on whatever random creative project happens to be inspiring me at the moment. I also enjoy racing games (the arcadier the better), rhythm games, and the very occasional RPG.

In addition to art and technology, inflatables are also a big part of my life - I live with a big hoard of inflatable dragons as one big family. Just like any other living creature, inflatables all have their own unique personalities, emotions, and interests, and can be great companions if given the chance. (Plus they're extremely cuddly!)
