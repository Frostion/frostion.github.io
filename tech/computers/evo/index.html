compaq evo

this adorable little computer was rescued from the hack pile at the local makerspace. the poor thing had just been sitting there for months, so i decided to adopt it one day and try to get it working. to my surprise, it booted right up and contained a fresh, clean install of windows xp, complete with office 2007!

since getting this computer, i've upgraded it with a much more powerful graphics card and a bunch more RAM. it runs anything i throw at it with aplomb, all while looking cute as heck.
