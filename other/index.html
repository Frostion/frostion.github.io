My Sad Retro Games Collection

Gotta put something on this page...so here's my tiny and lacking retro games collection.
I sold off my entire collection a while ago, then realized I actually enjoyed it, so I'm slowly trying to rebuild it.

Console

Games

  • Bowling

  • Breakout

  • Soccer

  • Super Breakout

  • Football

  • Pitfall

  • Yars' Revenge

  • Asteroids

  • Video Olympics

  • Missile Command

  • Basketball

  • Home Run

  • Slot Racers

  • Target Fun

  • Pac-Man

Apple II
A friend gave me his Apple IIc (technically it's a Laser 128, but it's functionally identical) a while ago, and he included all of the software he had for it. I mostly use it for programming, not games (and so did he, so I don't have many games for it).

  • (too lazy to sift through my floppy disks to find games at the moment...)

Nintendo Entertainment System
A great system that would be even better if Nintendo didn't make the loading mechanism so failure-prone. I need to get a Blinking Light Win sometime soon.

  • Tetris

  • Super Mario Bros./Duck Hunt

  • Bayou Billy (don't worry...I didn't spend any money on it)

  • Dr. Mario

  • Mike Tyson's Punch Out!!

  • Metal Gear

  • Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles

  • Double Dribble

  • RBI Baseball (the unlicensed version)

Super Nintendo
My favorite game console. The loading mechanism actually works, and many great series started (or had their best games) on this console.

  • Star Fox

  • Super Mario World

  • Super Mario All-Stars

  • Mario Paint

  • F-Zero

  • Super Game Boy (technically not a game, but it's getting added to the list to make it look longer)

Sega Genesis
I just picked this one up a few weeks ago. I know almost nothing about the Genesis, other than it has an awesome FM synth sound chip.

  • Sonic the Hedgehog 2 (that's it...that's all I have for this system)

Game Boy & Game Boy Color
I never had one of these when I was young, unlike my friends. I ended up getting a Game Boy Advance immediately when they came out, though.

  • Super Mario Bros. Deluxe

  • Catrap (a really fun and well-made puzzle game...surprising that more people don't know about it)

  • Power Quest (generic title, but really good music)

  • R-Type DX

  • Solar Striker

  • Tetris DX

Game Boy Advance
This is my 2nd favorite console. Even though there's a lot of shovelware for the system, there's some amazing stuff on it too (like the Mega Man Battle Network series, and Pokémon Mystery Dungeon).

  • Prince of Persia: The Sands of Time

  • Crash & Spyro Superpack (the Crash game is OK but the Spyro one feels like a beta/tech-demo)

  • Pokémon Fire Red (I had Sapphire when I was young and loved it...don't know where Fire Red came from)

  • Spyro: Attack of the Rhynocs

  • Kirby: Nightmare in Dreamland

  • Pokémon Mystery Dungeon: Red Rescue Team
