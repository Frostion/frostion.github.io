hyperunrealism

hyperunrealism is the name i've given my art style, as a direct contrast to hyperrealism. hyperrealism is all about capturing the look of reality, while hyperunrealism is all about capturing the feel of the reality that i wish existed instead. through hyperunrealism, i explore my personal thoughts, ideas, feelings, and realities created by the intersection of advanced technology, spirituality, and the edge of consciousness.

when creating artwork, i use affinity designer for the primary layout and graphics, bryce for 3D backgrounds, and processing for 2D backgrounds and effects.
