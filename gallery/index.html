hyperunrealism

Hyperunrealism is the name I've given my art style, as a direct contrast to hyperrealism. Hyperrealism is all about capturing the look of reality, while hyperunrealism is all about capturing the feel of the reality that I wish existed instead. It originated when I first started trying to emulate my favorite art style - metalheart - but I kept veering off in a different direction, so I decided to pursue that instead and see where it took me.
