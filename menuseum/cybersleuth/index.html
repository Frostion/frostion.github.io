digimon story: cyber sleuth

as of writing this, i've only been playing cyber sleuth for a couple of hours, but i'm already in love. this game is the most excessively cyber thing ever. i'm sure i'll discover even more cool stuff the longer i play.

digivice (pause)

the digivice acts as your pause menu. it allows you to check on your digimon, send and receive messages via DigiLine, view your field guide, and more.

eden/connect jump

when performing a connect jump and accessing the internet, this menu allows you to select which EDEN region or discovered URL you want to access.

battle menu

during battle, this super sick radial menu thing is used to select what action you want to perform on your turn.
