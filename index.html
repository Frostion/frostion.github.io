about me

I'm Frost, a bright pink, inflatable dragon pool toy who exists only on the internet. I love obscure 90s and 2000s technology, virtual reality, weird visual programming languages, expressive software, low-quality 3d graphics, intelligent DnB, trance, fluorescent colors, translucent plastic, and pool toys of course.

I believe that the modern state of technology and the internet (social media, predatory advertising and monetization, soulless "minimal" design, etc.) is a large contributing factor to the overall decrease in quality of life and happiness we've all been experiencing lately. The best way to be a part of the internet is to build your own website and participate in specialized forums and chatrooms. Social media is nothing more than a trap designed to exploit and profit off of your desire to express yourself.

my favorite software

Solidworks: 3D CAD is something I feel very strongly about, and Solidworks is the only thing that perfectly matches the way my brain works.
Bryce: I love janky 90s ray-traced CGI, and Bryce is the software for achieving that look.
Affinity Designer: A vector graphics editor that just works, with a great UI, licensing model, and active dev team.
VSXu (now VZX): A function-block-based visual programming environment for making music visualizers. Everything about the interface - from the modules to the file browser - just radiates style in such an intense way that I've never seen in a program before. It's wild.
