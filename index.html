about me

I'm Frost Sheridan, a pink inflatable dragon who exists only on the internet. I love obscure 90s and 2000s technology, virtual reality, software and UIs with wild expressive designs, weird computer graphics, intelligent DnB, trance, fluorescent colors, translucent plastic, and pool toys of course.

In my daily life, I enjoy using technology that feels the best to me personally, rather than what modern society dictates. If you come across a guy with a neon green Cybiko, a CD Walkman (that's also neon green), and an absolute brick of a feature phone, then you've probably found me. :P

I believe that the modern state of the web and consumer technology is a large contributing factor to the overall decrease in quality of life and happiness we've all been experiencing lately. The hyperfocus on social media, predatory advertising, exploitative monetization, and proliferation of soulless "minimal" design are all to blame. The best way to be a part of the internet is to build your own website and participate in specialized forums and chatrooms. Social media and the very idea of "discoverability" are nothing more than traps designed to exploit and profit off of your desire to express yourself.

my favorite software

Solidworks: 3D CAD is something I feel very strongly about, and Solidworks is the only thing that perfectly matches the way my brain works.
Bryce: I love the look of early ray-traced CGI, and Bryce is the software for achieving that look. Its interface and workflow is so much easier to understand than any other 3D software I've tried.
Affinity Designer: A vector graphics editor that actually works! It's got a great UI, a super active dev team
VSXu (now VZX): A function-block-based visual programming environment for making music visualizers. Everything about its visual design just radiates style in such an intense way that I've never seen in a program before.
